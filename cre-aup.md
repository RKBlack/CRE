# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) for Capital Real Estate and Asset Management

This Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) outlines the guidelines and expectations for the use of the computer systems and network provided by Capital Real Estate and Asset Management. By using our systems, you agree to abide by the following:

1. **Authorized Use**: Users are only allowed to access and use the systems and network for business-related activities directly associated with Capital Real Estate and Asset Management.

2. **Privacy and Security**: Users must respect the privacy and security of others. Unauthorized access, sharing of login credentials, or any attempt to bypass security measures is strictly prohibited.

3. **Data Protection**: Users are responsible for safeguarding sensitive information, including but not limited to, client data, financial records, and proprietary information. Unauthorized sharing or disclosure of such information is prohibited.

4. **Prohibited Content**: Users may not transmit, upload, or store any content that is unlawful, defamatory, offensive, or violates any applicable laws or regulations. This includes, but is not limited to, content that is discriminatory, harassing, or threatening.

5. **Malicious Activities**: Engaging in any form of malicious activity, including hacking, phishing, spreading malware, or attempting to disrupt the network, is strictly prohibited.

6. **Bandwidth and Resources**: Users must use network resources responsibly and avoid any activities that may degrade the performance for others.

7. **Copyright and Intellectual Property**: Users must respect copyright laws and may not reproduce, distribute, or share copyrighted material without proper authorization.

8. **Compliance with Policies**: Users are required to comply with all company policies, as well as any applicable local, state, and federal laws and regulations.

9. **Reporting Violations**: Any suspected violations of this policy must be reported to the IT department or management promptly.

10. **Consequences of Violations**: Violation of this AUP may result in disciplinary actions, including but not limited to, suspension or termination of access, and legal consequences as applicable.

By using the computer systems and network provided by Capital Real Estate and Asset Management, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to abide by this Acceptable Use Policy. Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary actions.

Thank you for your cooperation in maintaining a secure and productive computing environment.

- The Capital Real Estate and Asset Management Team
